SAIGON

Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam, named after the revolutionary leader. Locals often refer to Ho Chi Minh City as "Saigon" out of habit, as it is a shorter way to address the city. There are a total of 24 districts (Quậns) in Saigon. The main one is Q1 and the expat Q2 -Thảo Điền. It´s ok to get the sim at the airport. Book a grab to your accommodation. Withdraw money at vietcombank or ACB with revolut. 25k - 1€.

Enjoy your trip!!!! - Fran.
